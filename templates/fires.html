Predict the Total Acres that may be damaged in 2025

The prediction of the total area of fire burning in 2025 was calculated using a linear regression model trained on historical data. The model analyzed the relationship between the year and the total area burned in previous years. Based on this analysis, it estimated the total area of fire burning in 2025. The prediction provides valuable insights for planning and mitigating fire incidents in the future.
